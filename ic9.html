Indus - Icarus #9

Hip Hop

Firing accuracy for all the weapons have been revised to make them more viable when firing from the hip position.


Freeloader

Every weapon can now reload without exiting the ADS view, helping maintain target lock and resume firing ASAP!


Make it Rain

Loot items now physically interact with the world when dropped, giving them better visibility when performing specific inventory actions.


Sleek-n-Round

Visual progress indicators (UI) for certain players actions like consuming shields and medkits have been greatly simplified to prevent blocking combat-critical player view.


Follow Me!

Characters now have additional voice pings and commands to quickly relay combat critical info to squad-mates.
